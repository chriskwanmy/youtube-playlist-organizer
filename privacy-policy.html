Privacy Policy for YouTube Playlist Organizer

Last updated: March 2024

1. Information We Collect

YouTube Playlist Organizer collects and processes the following data:

  • YouTube account access token (for authentication purposes)
  • Your liked videos and playlist data from YouTube
  • Your video categorizations and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Authenticating with YouTube's API
  • Fetching your YouTube liked videos and playlists
  • Storing your video categorizations
  • Creating new playlists based on your categories

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. Your video categorizations and preferences are only stored locally and are not transmitted to any external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • YouTube Data API v3 - To access your YouTube data with your permission
  • Google OAuth 2.0 - For secure authentication

5. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All communication with YouTube's API is encrypted using HTTPS
  • Authentication is handled securely through Google's OAuth 2.0
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers beyond YouTube's API

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data (available in extension storage)
  • Delete your data (through extension's clear function)
  • Revoke YouTube access permissions at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
